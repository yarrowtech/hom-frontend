About Us

About House of Musa

House of Musa is an innovative crowdfunding platform designed to tackle the funding challenges faced by early-stage startups in India. We connect visionary startups with investors through both equity-based and no return-based crowdfunding, providing a secure, compliant, and user-friendly marketplace that simplifies capital raising. By removing traditional barriers such as strict eligibility criteria and slow approval processes, we make it easier for startups to access the funds they need. With AI-powered solutions, we offer personalized investment recommendations, risk profiling, and portfolio management, investors can make smarter, more informed decisions with low minimum investments. At House of Musa, we empower innovative startups and social enterprises, driving both economic growth and meaningful social impact across India.
